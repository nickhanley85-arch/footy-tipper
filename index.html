UX states

These are the product states the real app will need. Use the “Simulate late out” button above to trigger one of them in the detail screen.

Pending

Injury update pending

Show that the current tip may move once the next official list is refreshed.
Changed

Tip changed after late out

Make the change obvious, show the old tip, the new tip, and why it moved.
Complete

Review now available

Once a game finishes, switch the CTA from preview to review.
Empty

No data yet

Use a calm empty state for new rounds, preseason, or delayed source feeds.
